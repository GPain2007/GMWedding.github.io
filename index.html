Ecclesiastes 4: 9, 10, 12
Two are better than one because they have a good reward for their hard work. For if one of them falls, the other can help his partner up. But what will happen to the one who falls with no one to help him up? ... And someone may overpower one alone, but two together can take a stand against him. And a threefold cord cannot quickly be torn apart.

Y una cuerda tripleno se rompe fácilmente.
